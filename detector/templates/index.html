Goober Detector

Real-time Face Tracking with OpenCV

Face detection active - Look at the camera!

How it works:

  • This app uses OpenCV's Haar Cascade classifier to detect faces
  • Detected faces are highlighted with a green rectangle
  • The detection runs in real-time using your webcam
  • Click "Save Photo" to capture the current frame with face detection
  • View all saved photos in the gallery
  • Make sure your camera is enabled and you're in good lighting
